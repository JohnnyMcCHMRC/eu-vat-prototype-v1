Do you have a simplified invoice?

Simplified invoices (for example, till receipts) are shorter types of invoices that are usually up to £250.

Simplified invoices may not show net and VAT amounts separately.

If the invoice does not show the net amount and VAT amount separately, you must calculate the split yourself so you can complete the required fields.
