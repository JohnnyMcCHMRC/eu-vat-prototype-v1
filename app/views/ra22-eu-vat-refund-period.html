What is the refund period?

Your refund period must:

  • be in the past when you send your claim
  • not overlap with a period you have already claimed for
  • be within the same calendar year
  • be at least 3 months long, unless it ends in December

When is the refund period start date?

For example, 03 2008

When is the refund period end date?

For example, 08 2010
